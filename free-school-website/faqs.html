Few Questions you want answered

Why are we providing free websites to schools?
Our goal at Smile for dreams is to give as many schools as possible a free website. We believe that school's should have the opportunity to have a truly professional website, so we are doing it for no charge. We also understand that a school's budget is very tight and to save money they end up asking a teacher or secretary to build their site. In most cases, this costs schools plenty: software price, website functionality, overall appeal and more work for the teacher. Schools need to have more affordable options, and this is it.

Is it really free?
Yes, Smile for dreams does this as a community service. You will have to buy domain name and pay for hosting services only. There is no website development or maintenance charge from our side.

How much should I pay for the domain name?
Buying the domain name usually will cost you around Rs.500-600. You will have to renew this ervey year. Check more about Domain names here

How much should I pay for hosting the websites?
You can choose to host the webiste for free as well (Web hosting providers will add banners in your site). Else if you want add/banner free website then it will cost you around Rs. 500 per year. Read more about web hosting here

Who and how will I update the websites?
Once the initial setup is done by us, we will help you in updating the website and also we will provide the documentation and training to your shcool. And after that you can update the website by yourself. Remember, we are always there to help!!!
