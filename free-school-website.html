Who we Are

One of the initiative from "Smile4Dreams" is to provide the schools with a high quality, affordable website.

Our team consits of Elango (Smile4Dreams Founder) and Ashish. To know more about our team visit Our Team.

Both are passionate in web designing and their goal is to give schools a website that they can be proud of.

What we Do

"Smile4Dreams" provides free websites to schools. Below are some of the activities we will help you in
  • We will provide you with list of availble website templates. See the complete list here
  • We will help you in acquiring the domain name for the website. Know more about Domain name here
  • We will help you in hosting the website using web hosting. Know more about hosting services here
  • We will update the site with your content.
  • We will train your staff/student to make the updates to websites by yourselves in the future. We will also provide the web site maintenance kit.
  • We will always be available for any help!!!
Check out our frequently asked questionspage and if you don't get an answer to your question please contact us.
