FAQs

Tell me more about the Smile4Dreams?

Smile4Dreams strengthens the Indian community by making it easier for good people and good causes to connect. The site provides the platform using which the Orgnaisations can connect with the people who can provide support. Use this to help you do your part. Remember it all starts with You!!

Smile4Dreams is managed by the team of enthusiatic young and vibrant people.

How do I add a new non-profit organisation which is not listed here?

Have your own service project (Non-profit organization)? Use our Non Profit Registration page to add yours.

I applied for one of your programs more than 48 hours ago, and Im yet to hear back. What should I do?

Use our Website Feedback form and select "Non Profit Organisation Registration" in the drop down. We will get back to you ASAP.

Is Organisation from Chennai and Banglaore can only register here?

Currently we have added organisation from Chennai and Bangalore only. But if you are requesting then we will add yours as well.

How can I join the Smile4Dreams team?

We are most welcome to join our team, just use our Join Our Team page.

How can add Blogs or comments about particular organization?

Share your experiences with others so that the community of nonprofits can benefit from your knowledge, experience and opinions using our Blogs.
