How can you help!!

   Many of us have always nourished the thought of giving back to the society in any possible way. This is the opportunity to enjoy the joy of selfless giving. smile4dreams provides that unique platform for volunteers to serve the one in needy at your convenience; you can realize the power of teaching for underprivileged kids or to discover the joy of service in hospitals. After all

At the end of life we will not be judged by how many degrees we have received, how much money we have made, how many great things we have done.We will be judged by I was hungry, and you gave me something to eat, I was naked and you clothed me. I was homeless, and you took me in as said by Mother Theresa.


So do you have the willingness to follow your heart? Do you want to explore the real purpose of life? Do you want to go beyond your personal world to discover the potential of change you can bring to this society? Here are things you could do
1. Help the cause
-Use the Find Opportunities page and look for a cause that suits you.
-Share your experience with us which could inspire others as well.
-If you don't find a cause in your near by locality then inform us, we will find a suitable cause for you at your conviennt time.

2. Spread the word
-Get to know about the good people and good cause in your nearby locality
-Even if you are not residing in India, you can still help the cause by just spreading the word. Link our site smile4dreams or our official Facebook page on your website (check Spread the word page for more details).

3. Help us
- Get to know about our upcoming projects and join our team !!!Checkout Join our Team for more details.
- Be a volunteer contact for an organisation
- Contact us if you have an information/cause that needs to be shared
- Help us in updating our Database

At smile4dreams our motto is to leave this world as a better place and to bring more smiles. After all "Only a life lived for others is a life worth while."
